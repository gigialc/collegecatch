About Us

Welcome to CollegeCatch!

Making friends in college can be difficult enough, let alone meeting a potential partner. In Boston, regardles of what you're looking for - whether that's just a casual hookup or a serious relationship - finding that perfect person just isn't as easy as Elle Woods makes it out to be in Legally Blonde.


When it comes to dating in college, you have to be somewhat strategic. The days of stumbling into a hottie on your way to class and locking eyes over your fallen books are so over. But don't worry Boston singles! When you're looking for the best dating advice for college students in the area, CollegeCatch is where you'll find exactly what you need. Whether it's updates about the latest social events, tips on how to up your online dating game, or examples of great conversation starters - we have you covered!
