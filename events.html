Fun Boston Date Night Recs!

If you're looking for something fun to do with a partner in the Boston area, try these student friendly options:

  • Most Italian restaurants in the North End - there's a type of pasta for everyone.
  • Take a tour of the MFA or the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum - students in the area usually get discounts or even free entry!
  • Have a picnic at one of Boston's many green spaces: you can try the Arnold Arboretum or the Esplanade!
  • Try a night of bowling or arcade games at Lucky Strike!
